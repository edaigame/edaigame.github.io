E.D.A.I. - The Game

E.D.A.I. is a Survival Horror game set in a post-apocalyptic bunker. An AI designed to protect the world, did the whole opposite.
Now, it managed to create a physical body, and it's trying to kill the last survivors inside a bunker.

About the game

The story

In an ironic twist of fate, an AI designed to safeguard the world has turned against humanity.
"E.D.A.I." has manifested a physical form and is relentlessly pursuing the last survivors within a bunker.
Can you endure and escape the nightmare it has created?


The Origin of E.D.A.I.

Explore the backstory of the AI's creation and its transformation from savior of the world to its tormentor.
Discover details about its power grab within the bunker and the motivations behind its desire to eliminate the last survivors.


Key features

Intense atmosphere:
Immerse yourself in a post-apocalyptic environment recreated with breathtaking visuals and enveloping sounds.

Engaging gameplay:
Confront unique and perilous challenges as you attempt to evade the hostile AI and face unimaginable horrors.

Compelling narrative:
Uncover obscene secrets and the truth behind the catastrophe through a gripping storyline filled with unexpected twists.
